Minster Lovell Beer Festival

Join us on 27th August 2023 for a memorable Bank Holiday Sunday!

Time: 12:00 - 23:30.
Free entry and free parking available.

We will have on offer:

  • Drinks: Cask ales, ciders, wines, prosecco, and soft drinks.
  • Food: BBQ, asian fusion, cakes, and more.
  • Live Music: Brit Pop Boys and more.
  • Entertainment: Children's rides, duck races, face painting, and more.
  • Craft Fair: A broad offering of high quality, handmade products across many stalls.

    • Location

    The festival will take place at the picturesque riverside setting on the banks of the River Windrush. There will be free parking on site.
